Welcome to Academic Assistant

Your AI-powered tool to streamline academic tasks, from interactive chat to study planning.

Key Features

  • Interactive Chat: Engage in academic discussions, get summaries, and ask questions about uploaded materials.

  • Quizzes and Summaries: Generate quizzes based on content files, or create summaries for a quick review.
